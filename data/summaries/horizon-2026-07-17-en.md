The project used approximately $25,000 worth of Claude Opus and Fable tokens, but leveraged a Claude Max subscription to reduce actual cost. The Wisp protocol is a method for proxying multiple network connections over a single WebSocket, which is essential for circumventing browser security that blocks direct network access from Wasm code.
